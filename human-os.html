Nuel Core: Human OS

A structured productivity and automation framework designed to optimize focus, eliminate distractions, automate routines, and improve operational discipline through digital systems engineering.

Project Overview

Nuel Core: Human OS was designed as a personal operating framework focused on improving productivity, consistency, and mental clarity through automation, structured workflows, and measurable habit systems.

The project combines deep work protocols, movement integration, hydration tracking, dashboard systems, and automation workflows into a centralized mobile productivity environment.

Core System Components

Deep Work Engine

Structured 90-minute focus sessions completed twice daily, targeting a minimum of 180 minutes of intentional project execution.

Hydration System

A daily hydration monitoring protocol maintaining approximately 128 oz of water intake for cognitive performance and sustained energy.

Automation Infrastructure

Built using MacroDroid, KWGT widgets, custom dashboards, and mobile automation systems to centralize workflows.

System Integrity Score

A routine tracking system designed to maintain operational consistency at a target completion rate of 90%.

The 90-Day Framework

Month 1 — Hardware Calibration

Eliminating digital distractions, establishing structured routines, implementing reset protocols, and building foundational discipline.

Month 2 — Custom Integration

Building dashboards, integrating productivity systems, syncing workflows, and introducing automation logic.

Month 3 — System Autonomy

Achieving operational flow, automating repetitive processes, and focusing on high-level execution and leadership development.
